Welcome to BitsNBytes Coding Club!

BitsNBytes is an Educational Club started by the faculties of JIT under ISE Department for all the students. The Club was founded on 4th April 2021 during the pandemic to conduct online workshop for the students. The club has more than 50 students in the learning process under their faculties. The club improves the knowledge of the students enhancing their problem solving capabilities. It also Encourages and Empowers students to learn various coding principles and enable them to be industry ready.

Our team of volunteers is extremely friendly and helpful who will help make this a comfortable experience for you. They are skilled and specialized in various domains. This team also includes faculties of JIT who are always ready to help you upskill and learn vital technologies.

The Club invites various Technology Experts and Successful Entrepreneurs for it's workshops. It helps the students to step into the right path towards the Success.

If you are not yet Registered, then Register for the club from the Navigation menu. Registered students get free access to the club's drive and Events Participation.

We are looking forward to meet you!
